Tab Splitter Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

Tab Splitter is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information Collection and Use

Tab Splitter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

3. Local Storage

Tab Splitter uses Chrome's local storage to save your preferences and settings. This data is stored on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

4. Permissions

Tab Splitter requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To modify the content of the active tab for splitting functionality.
  • scripting: To inject necessary scripts for tab splitting and manipulation.
  • storage: To save your preferences locally.

These permissions are used solely for the functioning of the extension and do not involve data collection or transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Tab Splitter does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

6. Data Security

As Tab Splitter does not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security risks associated with its use.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
